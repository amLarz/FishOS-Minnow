
## Plan
### Introduction:
FishOS Minnow is a more passive game that requires players to wait in order to catch fish. Style of gameplay is inspired by Tamagotchi, games with fishing (just fishing in general) and other games that have wait times before events calling for action happen. In this case, when you cast your rod you will enter a phase where you will have to wait for a fish to bite. There is a choice to leave this state but you will get nothing. This is like fishing games but making it a little bit more real with a longer wait time as more you progress the game. Furthermore, rarer fish appear in longer wait times. I have an idea where you can cast your rod in different depths and in each increasing the time and the possible fish caught. This will be followed with the logic that you may *catch any other fish that came before that depth*. Increasing the rarity of the fish the deeper you get. This will be the framework of the progression.
